Presentation 3

A United Center

The Power of Collective Spaces

Session Summary

In response to the prevalence of divided centers, this presentation highlights collective spaces as integral sources of connection, growth, and change. Featuring the perspectives of writing and STEM peer tutors, we'll showcase the benefits of uplifting these spaces to bring about positive changes in campuses of all shapes and structures.
