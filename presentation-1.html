Presentation 1

A Case for Communications Labs

Politics, Anti-Intellectualism, and Gutting

Session Summary

Inspired by the gutting of Communications departments at the UC level, this presentation underlines the importance of effective Communications tutoring when student advocacy, critical thinking skills, and public speaking skills are under attack. This presentation offers diverse perspectives on the discipline while offering pragmatic solutions to academics.
